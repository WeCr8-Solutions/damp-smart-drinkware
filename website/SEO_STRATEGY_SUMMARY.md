## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks
1. **Trend Dependency**: Damp drinking trend may fade
2. **Keyword Cannibalization**: Multiple pages targeting similar keywords
3. **Content Quality**: Maintaining high-quality, original content

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Diversified Keywords**: Focus on broader drink tracking terms
2. **Clear Page Hierarchy**: Distinct purposes for each page
3. **Regular Updates**: Keep content fresh and relevant
4. **User Intent Focus**: Always prioritize user value over keyword density
