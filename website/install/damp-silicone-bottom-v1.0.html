Installation Guide: DAMP Silicone Bottom v1.0

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Unbox your DAMP Silicone Bottom v1.0.
  2. Ensure the bottom of your drinkware is clean and dry.
  3. Attach the silicone bottom securely to your drinkware.
  4. Download the DAMP app and pair the silicone bottom via Bluetooth.
