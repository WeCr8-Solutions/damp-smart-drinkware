## What is DAMP?
DAMP (Drink Abandonment Monitoring Protocol) is a revolutionary smart drinkware technology that prevents users from leaving their beverages behind. Using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) tracking technology, DAMP products attach to any existing drinkware and provide real-time location monitoring through a mobile app.

## Core Value Proposition
"Never Leave Your Drink Behind"

DAMP solves the universal problem of abandoned beverages by providing:
- Real-time drink location tracking
- Smart zone detection (home, office, car)
- Mobile app notifications and reminders
- Universal compatibility with all drinkware brands
- Long battery life (6+ months)
- Waterproof design (IP67 rating)

## Common Questions

### How does DAMP work?
DAMP devices use Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology to communicate with your smartphone. When you move away from your drink beyond a set distance, the app sends you a notification reminder.

### Is it compatible with my drinkware?
Yes! DAMP products are designed to be universally compatible with ALL drinkware brands including YETI, Stanley, Hydro Flask, Starbucks cups, Contigo, Ember, and any other mug, tumbler, or water bottle.

### How long does the battery last?
6+ months on a single replaceable coin cell battery (CR2032).

### Is it waterproof?
Yes, all DAMP products have an IP67 waterproof rating, meaning they can be fully submerged in water.
