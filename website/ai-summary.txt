## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

### Product Questions

**Q: How does DAMP work?**
A: DAMP devices use Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE 5.0) technology to communicate with your smartphone. When you move away from your drink beyond a set distance (customizable: 50-400 feet), the app sends you a push notification reminder. The device also tracks your drink's location on a map and monitors hydration patterns.

**Q: Is DAMP compatible with my drinkware?**
A: Yes! DAMP is designed for universal compatibility. It works with 100+ drinkware brands including YETI, Stanley, Hydro Flask, Starbucks cups, Contigo, Ember, and any other mug, tumbler, or water bottle. If it holds liquid, DAMP can track it.

**Q: How long does the battery last?**
A: The DAMP Handle and Silicone Bottom use replaceable coin cell batteries (CR2032/CR2450) that last 6-15 months depending on usage. The Cup Sleeve has a rechargeable battery (USB-C) that lasts 4 weeks per charge. You'll receive low battery warnings 30 days in advance.

**Q: Is DAMP waterproof?**
A: Yes! All DAMP products have an IP67 or IP68 waterproof rating, meaning they can be fully submerged in water. The Handle and Bottom can be submerged up to 3 feet for 30 minutes, and the Cup Sleeve is fully submersible. They're all dishwasher safe (top rack recommended).

**Q: What is the tracking range?**
A: The tracking range varies by product:
- DAMP Handle: Up to 400 feet (122 meters)
- Cup Sleeve: Up to 300 feet (91 meters)
- Silicone Bottom: Up to 250 feet (76 meters)
These are line-of-sight ranges; walls and obstacles will reduce range but you'll still get 150-200 feet in most indoor environments.

**Q: Does DAMP require a subscription?**
A: No! All basic features are free forever, including location tracking, abandonment alerts, zone detection, and basic hydration tracking. We offer an optional DAMP Premium subscription ($2.99/month) for advanced features like unlimited zones, family sharing, and detailed analytics, but it's completely optional.

**Q: How do I attach DAMP to my drinkware?**
A: It depends on the product:
- **Handle**: Clips onto the side or handle of your drinkware (fits 2.5"-4" diameter)
- **Cup Sleeve**: Slides over your mug or tumbler like a traditional sleeve
- **Silicone Bottom**: Simply place it underneath your drink on any flat surface
All attachments are secure but removable - no permanent modification to your drinkware.

**Q: Can I track multiple drinks at once?**
A: Yes! The free plan allows you to track up to 3 drinks simultaneously. DAMP Premium allows unlimited drink tracking - perfect for families or people with multiple bottles.

**Q: Does DAMP work with voice assistants?**
A: Yes! DAMP integrates with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri. You can ask:
- "Alexa, where's my water bottle?"
- "Hey Google, find my coffee mug"
- "Siri, show me my YETI location"

**Q: What if I leave my phone behind?**
A: DAMP has an "Offline Mode" that stores location data when your phone is out of range. When you return within range, the data syncs automatically. You'll see the last known location of your drink.

**Q: Can I share drink locations with family?**
A: Yes! DAMP Premium includes Family Sharing for up to 6 family members. Everyone can see the locations of shared drinks in real-time - perfect for parents tracking kids' water bottles or couples sharing drinkware.
